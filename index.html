How Dazzler Works

Download Dazzler app from Playstore.

You connect your smartwatch to this app via bluetooth, the smartwatch uses every available sensor (happimeter) in it to scan your mood every minutes, then send the data to the app.

The app picks up the data and create a playlist according to the data sent (sad, happy or neutral).

All you need is just a smart watch and an Andriod Phone and whoola!
